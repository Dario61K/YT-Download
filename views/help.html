YT Download

How to use

1- Enter the YouTube URL:

  • Open Youtube
  • Find The video you want to download and copy its URL
  • Go back to YT Download

2- Display video information:

  • Paste the URL into the search bar.
  • Wait a few seconds while the video information is processed. You’ll see details such as the title, thumbnail, and available quality options.

3- Choose video quality (optional):

  • Some videos may offer different quality options (e.g., 360p, 720p, etc.).
  • Select your desired quality if available. Keep in mind that not all formats may be available due to YouTube’s policies.

4- Download the video:

  • Click the download button.
  • Wait for the file to process and download to your device.
  • The runtime may vary depending on the file size and your internet speed. Be patient!

Note: Make sure you have storage space on your device
